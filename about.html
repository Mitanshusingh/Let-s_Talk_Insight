Dr. Nitu Dansinghani

Licensed Psychologist • Specializations: CBT, Couples Therapy, Child & Adolescent Therapy

Professional Summary

With extensive experience in clinical psychology, Dr. Nitu Dansinghani is dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based care to individuals, couples, and families. She holds specialized certifications in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and trauma-informed care, helping clients navigate life's complexities, overcome anxiety and depression, and build lasting resilience.

Approach & Values

Therapy is a collaborative journey. Dr. Dansinghani cultivates a safe, non-judgmental, and highly confidential environment where clients feel truly heard. Your first session will focus on understanding your unique story, identifying your goals, and establishing a tailored roadmap for healing and growth.
